Welcome to Journey Capture, a powerful Chrome extension designed for testers, developers, and anyone who needs to document and map out complex web interactions. Capture screenshots, annotate directly on images, log system information, and track network activity all in one tool. Whether you're identifying bugs or documenting journeys for future reference, Journey Capture makes it easy.

Key Features:

  • Create Journeys: Capture multiple web pages and organise them into a single journey. Track the progress and interactions that matter.
  • Screenshot Annotation: Use the built-in pencil tool to annotate screenshots. Highlight areas of interest or bugs with ease and choose from a range of colours.
  • System Information Logging: Automatically log key system data (OS, screen resolution, browser version, cookies, etc.) when capturing pagesâ€”perfect for detailed documentation.
  • Network Log Tracking: Capture network logs in real time. Sort, filter, and search through logs to understand page performance, errors, and potential bottlenecks.
  • Export/Import Journeys: Share your captured journeys with teammates by exporting them to a JSON file. Import saved journeys to continue annotations or further exploration.

Why Use Journey Capture?

  • Exploratory Testing: Track user interactions and site performance during exploratory tests, capture system states, and log vital information for debugging.
  • Bug Reporting: Share precise captures with annotated screenshots and network logs to help your team resolve issues faster.
  • Collaborate: Easily share journeys and collaborate across teams by importing and exporting data. Work together to achieve a more polished web experience.

How It Works:

  • Start a Journey: Click on "Start Capture" and begin capturing web pages.
  • Add Titles & Notes: Organise your captures with optional titles and detailed notes for later review.
  • Annotate: Use the pencil tool to highlight key areas on each screenshot.
  • View Logs: Review system information and network logs to identify patterns or issues.
  • Save and Share: Export journeys to JSON files and share them with your team or import them back into the extension for future updates.
